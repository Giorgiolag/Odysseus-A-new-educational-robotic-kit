**Note on Kit Completeness:** It is important to highlight that this total cost is highly comprehensive. It covers not only the core programmable electronics, power management, and 3D printed chassis (Part A), but also a complete "ecosystem" of interactive components (Part B). The BOM is deliberately provisioned with enough DC motors, micro servos, sensors, and LEDs so that **every available modular slot on the robot can be populated simultaneously**. This ensures that each manufactured kit provides a fully functional, highly versatile platform right out of the box, without requiring any supplemental component purchases.

**Note on Cost Calculation & Kit Completeness:** The estimated cost of **€46.02 per unit** is derived by taking the total manufacturing cost for a batch of 20 kits and dividing it by 20. This batch-calculation approach provides a highly realistic and reproducible cost estimate, as many electronic components and raw materials (e.g., SMD passive components, pin headers, solder paste, and PCB fabrication) are subject to Minimum Order Quantities (MOQs) or are sold exclusively in bulk packages. Calculating the cost of a single isolated kit would be misleading and impractical for real-world reproduction.
